About

Deutsche Digital builds and contributes to the emergence and growth of digital ecosystems as investors and advisors. It draws on the experience of the creation of successful innovation hotspots in Berlin and Beersheba combining tech ventures, established companies, and investors.

Deutsche Digital acts as an early-stage investor for start-ups to grow and as an independent advisor and partner to boards of established companies to transform. Building on the technological disruption for factor 10 value growth.
